# Vellum Pitch Deck Outline

## 1. Title slide
### Title
Vellum

### Subtitle
The control layer for buy-side operations

### Supporting line
Rules-based, AI-enhanced workflow automation across OMS, IBOR, ABOR, and CBOR environments.

## 2. The problem
Buy-side operations still depend on fragmented systems, manual reconciliations, and brittle workflows.

Common pain points:
- disconnected OMS, IBOR, ABOR, and CBOR views
- timing and lifecycle mismatches
- quantity, cash, and valuation breaks
- corporate-action and reference-data errors
- manual exception triage
- weak evidence and signoff workflows
- high operational and reimbursement risk

## 3. Why existing approaches fall short
Traditional approaches create too much overhead:
- static books of record
- batch-oriented processing
- manual reconciliations
- fragmented workflow tooling
- generic dashboards without operational control logic
- legacy IBOR-heavy architectures that are expensive and rigid

## 4. The solution
Vellum is a rules-based, AI-enhanced workflow and control platform for buy-side firms.

Vellum:
- integrates OMS, IBOR, ABOR, and CBOR data
- normalizes that data into canonical contracts
- applies proprietary deterministic rules
- classifies meaningful exceptions
- orchestrates workflow routing, ownership, and signoff
- uses RAG, LLMs, and agentic workflows to accelerate investigation and resolution

## 5. Product architecture
### Authority order
1. Canonical contracts and data dictionary
2. Deterministic rules
3. Workflow orchestration and control objects
4. Retrieval (RAG)
5. LLM explanation, drafting, and agentic assistance

### Core product principle
Rules decide. Workflows act. AI assists.

## 6. Core use cases
Vellum detects and routes:
- timing mismatch
- lifecycle mismatch
- reference / enrichment mismatch
- quantity mismatch
- cash mismatch
- valuation mismatch
- corporate-action mismatch
- missing-record mismatch
- signoff / governance state mismatch

## 7. Why now
Three forces are converging:
- buy-side firms are under pressure to do more with leaner operations teams
- legacy operating models remain fragmented and manual
- modern AI finally makes retrieval, drafting, and guided investigation genuinely useful when grounded in deterministic controls

## 8. Business value
Vellum helps firms:
- reduce manual reconciliation effort
- identify breaks earlier
- improve workflow governance
- preserve evidence and lineage
- accelerate exception resolution
- reduce dependence on brittle IBOR-heavy architectures

## 9. Go-to-market wedge
Initial wedge:
- asset managers and asset owners
- middle- and back-office operational controls
- workflows that connect investment intent to downstream operational outcome
- initial focus on exception detection, routing, investigation, and governed resolution

## 10. Vision
The long-term vision is not just a better reconciliation tool.

It is a modern control layer for buy-side operations that can replace brittle IBOR-heavy operating models with a more flexible combination of:
- canonical contracts
- deterministic rules
- workflow orchestration
- evidence capture
- AI-enhanced operator assistance
