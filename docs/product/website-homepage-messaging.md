## Problem section
### Headline
Buy-side operations still run on fragmented books, manual reconciliations, and brittle workflows.

### Body
Most firms still depend on disconnected OMS, IBOR, ABOR, and CBOR environments to manage the path from investment intent to operational outcome. That creates timing gaps, lifecycle mismatches, data-quality issues, and a constant stream of manual exception work.

The result is slower resolution, weaker controls, and unnecessary operational and reimbursement risk.

## Solution section
### Headline
Vellum replaces brittle operating processes with a rules-based, AI-enhanced control layer.

### Body
Vellum integrates operational data across the buy-side stack, applies deterministic rules to detect meaningful breaks, orchestrates workflows to route and resolve exceptions, and uses AI to accelerate investigation, retrieval, and drafting.

Instead of asking teams to sift through every mismatch, Vellum helps them focus on the exceptions that actually matter.

## How it works
### 1. Integrate the operating stack
Connect OMS, IBOR, ABOR, and CBOR data into canonical contracts.

### 2. Apply deterministic controls
Use proprietary rules to identify timing, lifecycle, quantity, cash, valuation, corporate-action, and governance breaks.

### 3. Orchestrate workflows
Automatically route exceptions to the right owner, queue, and signoff path.

### 4. Accelerate resolution with AI
Use RAG, LLMs, and agentic workflows to retrieve evidence, explain breaks, draft communications, and support operator investigation.
